How Subsidies Strain our Economy

As Americans struggle to afford food and housing for themselves and their families, their taxes continue to serve as a crutch for people too lazy to stand on their feet.

For example, it may surprise you that 24% of SNAP recipients are overweight, and 54% are obese.
